About the Band

We are a group of four who have all played in great bands — coming together to form the ultimate country rock party band. Ready to take Houston by storm, Space City Crasher brings the songs you grew up with back to life, night after night.

From Garth Brooks to Lynyrd Skynyrd, George Strait to the Eagles — if it had a killer guitar riff or made you want to two-step, we play it. Loud, proud, and right.
